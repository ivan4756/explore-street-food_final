Privacy Policy

1. Overview

At Explore Street Food we respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide (e.g., email for newsletter or contact forms).
  • Non-personal information such as browser type, device, pages visited, and approximate location (via analytics or your browser).
  • Cookies/local storage used for features like favourites, consent choices, and basic analytics.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the Website and its features.
  • Respond to messages and support requests.
  • Send optional updates if you subscribe to our newsletter (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Monitor aggregate usage to understand what content is useful.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us run the Website (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality obligations, or if required by law.

5. Data Retention & Security

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

  • Opt out of the newsletter via the unsubscribe link.
  • Clear or block cookies in your browser (some features may not work).
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

7. Children

The Website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

8. Third-Party Links

Our pages may link to third-party sites (e.g., maps, social media). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated date below.
