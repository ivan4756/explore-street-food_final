Malaysian Street Food

Discover the rich flavors of Malaysian hawker stalls.

Nasi Lemak Rice

Malaysia's national dish with fragrant coconut rice, sambal, anchovies, peanuts, and egg.

Satay Skewers

Grilled skewers of marinated meat served with peanut sauce.

Char Kway Teow Noodles

Flat rice noodles stir-fried with prawns, egg, and bean sprouts in a smoky wok hei flavor.

Curry Laksa Noodles

A popular Malaysian noodle soup, includes a rich, coconut milk-based broth infused with curry spices, along with noodles, proteins like prawns or chicken, tofu puffs, and garnishes and herbs.

Roti Canai Flatbread

Flaky flatbread usually served with dhal curry or chicken curry.

Teh Tarik Drink

Malaysia's favorite “pulled tea” with creamy frothy top, perfect with street food.

Cendol Dessert

A refreshing dessert consisting of shaved ice, coconut milk, green rice flour jelly, and palm sugar syrup.

Keropok Lekor Snack

A traditional Malay fish cracker snack made from fish and sago flour and seasoned with salt and sugar.
